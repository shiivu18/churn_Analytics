Customer retention is one of the most critical challenges faced by subscription-based businesses such as telecom companies, SaaS platforms, banks, and e-commerce services. Acquiring a new customer is significantly more expensive than retaining an existing one, yet many companies struggle to identify customers who are likely to discontinue their services. This results in revenue loss, reduced customer lifetime value, and inefficient marketing and retention efforts.

The objective of this project is to develop an end-to-end Customer Churn Analytics & Prediction System that helps businesses identify customers at high risk of churn and understand the key factors contributing to customer attrition. The system will analyze historical customer data, uncover behavioral and financial patterns associated with churn, and build a machine learning model capable of predicting future churn probability.

This project is designed for telecom industry customer data, where each customer record contains information such as demographics, subscribed services, contract type, payment method, monthly charges, tenure, and churn status. By performing data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based business analysis, feature engineering, and predictive modeling, the system aims to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights.

The primary stakeholders of this solution are:

Business Analysts seeking customer behavior insights
Retention Teams aiming to reduce churn rates
Marketing Teams targeting at-risk customers with personalized offers
Management Teams monitoring revenue impact and customer retention KPIs

The project will provide measurable outcomes including:

Identification of customer segments with the highest churn risk
Quantification of revenue at risk due to churn
Prediction of churn probability using machine learning models
Visualization of churn trends and KPIs through an interactive dashboard
Business recommendations for improving customer retention strategies

The final system will include a deployed interactive dashboard where users can explore churn metrics, analyze customer segments, and predict whether a customer is likely to churn based on input attributes. The success of the project will be measured using machine learning evaluation metrics such as Precision, Recall, F1-Score, and AUC-ROC, along with business metrics such as churn rate reduction potential and estimated revenue savings.

This project demonstrates the practical application of data analytics, machine learning, SQL, and dashboard development to solve a real-world business problem with measurable organizational impact.
